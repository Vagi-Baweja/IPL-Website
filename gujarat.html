Gujarat Titans

Gujarat Titans is a professional franchise cricket team based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. The Titans compete in the Indian Premier League. Founded in 2021, Gujarat Titans' home ground is Narendra Modi Stadium in Motera. The franchise is owned by CVC Capital Partners.
